iOS APPS

I created this app because I love Circus Smirkus and I wanted to learn how to develop iPhone/iPad apps. What better way to learn then a project for an amazing not for profit company. That was v0.1. v1.0 was done as my Capstone at Wheaton College (MA). Currently, with over 2,000 downloads the app is at v1.2 with v1.3 on the way. I use technologies such as geolocation to give users directions from their current location to whatever show site they would like to go to. Using REST I gather data from Smirkus’ Facebook and Youtube channels to get news and videos to keep my users up-to-date with the latest Smirkus Info. You can even buy your tickets to a show.

Visit appstore.com/circussmirkus for your free download of this app for iPhone or iPad. Once you’ve downloaded it, don’t forget to check out when Circus Smirkus will be in a town near you.

cowDuck is an app available for the iPhone. Working in a team of four I was responsible for the overall UI as well as food section of the app. cowDuck was created for the Wheaton College (MA) community as a resource for the most essential things for students on campus. The app features a campus map and student/teacher directory, a course catalogue, all up-to-date campus dining hall and on campus cafe menus. Transportation timetables and campus events.

I have a cron job running on a server that periodically scrubs the campus dining website for the most current menu data. Using REST the app talks to that server to make sure it always has the most current and up-to-date menu information for breakfast, lunch or dinner.

You can download the free app here: appstore.com/cowduck
